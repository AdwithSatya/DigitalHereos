# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Groups of friends, housemates, travelers, or colleagues who share expenses and need to settle up quickly. Primary context: just finished dinner / a trip / a shared purchase; phone in hand, want to split fast and move on.

## Product Purpose

A lightweight, browser-only bill splitter. Users add a bill, add participants, pick a split mode (equal, custom amount, percentage, or itemized), and instantly see who owes whom — with the minimum number of transactions needed to settle up. No accounts, no servers, no friction.

## Brand Personality

Precise, friendly, instant. Like a sharp pocket calculator that also knows your friends' names.

## Anti-references

- Splitwise (over-engineered, requires account, slow)
- Generic form-heavy tools (bureaucratic feel)
- SaaS-cream AI clones (warm-neutral bg + dusty-brown primary)
- Navy-and-gold fintech aesthetic

## Design Principles

1. **Zero friction** — the primary task (split a bill) should take under 30 seconds.
2. **Trust the math** — every result must feel obviously correct. Show working, not just answers.
3. **Disappear into the task** — UI components should feel familiar; delight is in the speed, not the decoration.
4. **Honest states** — empty states teach; error states recover; loading states are instant or skeleton, never spinners blocking content.
5. **One screen at a time** — progressive disclosure: don't show settlement details until the split is configured.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA minimum (4.5:1 body, 3:1 large text)
- Reduced-motion alternative for all transitions
- Keyboard navigable; all interactive elements focusable
- Clear labels on all inputs; no color-only state signals
